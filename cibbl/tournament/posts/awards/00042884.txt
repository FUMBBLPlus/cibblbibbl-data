Crushing Victory
Awarded whenever a team scores at least 4 times, while the opponent scores no touchdowns at all. Worth 1 Prestige Point to the team, but will 'decay' like normally earned Prestige Points. (Already added in calculations.)
- Lords of the Silent Death (in match vs Secret Cult of Shiamoto)


Fame Killer
Awarded to player killing an induced Star Player. Worth 1 Prestige Point to the team as long as that player is on the team.
- Sam Sin (Ninja Warriors) fouled Zara the Slayer to death in match versus Grand Celestial Delegate


Mean Scoring Machine
Awarded to player scoring 3+ touchdowns in one match. Worth 1 Prestige Point to the team as long as that player is on the team.
- Nightmaster Skirshakk (Lords of the Silent Death) (in match vs Secret Cult of Shiamoto)


Oriental Aesthetics
Awarded to best players of Cathay and Nipponspring regionals. Worth 1 Prestige Point to the team as long as that player is on the team.
- After the Lotus Flowers Have Opened (Grand Celestial Delegate) (Touchdowns, SPP)
- Sam Sin (Ninja Warriors) (Fouls)


Star Player
Awarded to any player reaching 51+ SPP. Player is recognized as one of the absolute stars of the league. Worth 1 Prestige Point to the team as long as that player is on the team.
- After the Lotus Flowers Have Opened (Grand Celestial Delegate)
- Jessie Graff (Ninja Warriors)
