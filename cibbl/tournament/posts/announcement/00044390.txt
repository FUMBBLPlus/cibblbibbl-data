Youngblood Scramble



Location: Home & Away stadiums of teams.

Participants:
- The Youngblood Scramble, a larger tournament for new teams.

Expected entrants: 8.

Tournament format: Swiss, 5 rounds.

Special rules:
- None

Prestige Points are awarded as follows:
• Youngblood Scramble 4th place: +1
• Youngblood Scramble 3rd place: +2
• Youngblood Scramble 2nd place: +5
• Youngblood Scramble 1st place: +8
