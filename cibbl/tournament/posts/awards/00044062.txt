Fame Killer
Awarded to player killing an induced Star Player. Worth 1 Prestige Point to the team as long as that player is on the team.
- Saku (Secret Cult Of Shiamoto) killed (by foul) Glart Smashrip Jr. in match versus Lords of the Silent Death


Oriental Aesthetics
Awarded to best players of Cathay and Nippon spring regionals. Worth 1 Prestige Point to the team as long as that player is on the team.
- Shiamoto The Undying (Secret Cult Of Shiamoto) (Casualties, SPP)
- Kacy Cataranzo (Ninja Warriors) (Fouls)
- Ronin Headchopper (Secret Cult Of Shiamoto) (Blocking Scorer)


Star Player
Awarded to any player reaching 51+ SPP. Player is recognized as one of the absolute stars of the league. Worth 1 Prestige Point to the team as long as that player is on the team.
- Shiamoto The Undying (Secret Cult Of Shiamoto)
- Ronin Swift Wind (Secret Cult Of Shiamoto)


Target Eliminated
Awarded when a player kills another player that has earned at least one Achievement worth at least 1 prestige point. Worth 1 Prestige Point to the team as long as that player is on the team.
- Mirror of the Sky (Grand Celestial Delegate) killed Jessie Graf in match vs Ninja Warriors
- Nightlord Azhiskikk (Lords of the Silent Death) killed (by tackle) Daniel Gil in match vs Ninja Warriors


Triple X
Awarded to player hitting 3+ casualties in one match. Worth 1 Prestige Point to the team as long as that player is on the team.
- After the Lotus Flowers Have Opened (Grand Celestial Delegate) in match vs Secret Cult Of Shiamoto
