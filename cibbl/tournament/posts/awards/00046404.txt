Crushing Victory
Awarded whenever a team scores at least 4 times, while the opponent scores no touchdowns at all. Worth 1 Prestige Point to the team, but will 'decay' like normally earned Prestige Points. (Already added in calculations.)
- Trantio Dragon Hawks (in match vs Bordeleaux Chevaliers)


Mean Scoring Machine
Awarded to player scoring 3+ touchdowns in one match. Worth 1 Prestige Point to the team as long as that player is on the team.
- Tabaldin (Trantio Dragon Hawks) in match versus Bordeleaux Chevaliers


Old World Values
Awarded to best players of the Old World Regionals. Worth 1 Prestige Point to the team as long as that player is on the team.
- Tabaldin (Trantio Dragon Hawks) (Touchdowns)
- Alexei Razumovski (Ursun Throat Singers) (Interceptions)
- Vicente Hierromano (Montetriste Noblemen) (Casualties)
- Conrrart de Grantrue (Bordeleaux Chevaliers) (SPP, Scoring Thrower, Blocking Thrower, Triple) (achievement awarded post-mortem)
- Cassiopea Canova (Trantio Dragon Hawks) (Fouls)


Star Player
Awarded to any player reaching 51+ SPP. Player is recognized as one of the absolute stars of the league. Worth 1 Prestige Point to the team as long as that player is on the team.
- Tabaldin (Trantio Dragon Hawks)


Super Star Player
Awarded to any player reaching 76+ SPP. Player is recognized as a super star of the league. Worth 1 Prestige Point to the team as long as that player is on the team.
- Manuel Grandegolpe (Montetriste Noblemen)
- Tabaldin (Trantio Dragon Hawks)
- Jared "the Prince" (Stone Prince’s Men)


Target Eliminated
Awarded when a player kills another player that has earned at least one Achievement worth at least 1 prestige point. Worth 1 Prestige Point to the team as long as that player is on the team.
- Stenka Pugachev (Ursun Throat Singers) killed Khirathe in this match versus Trantio Dragon Hawks)
- Vicente Hierromano (Montetriste Noblemen) killed Barda, the secret Sister in this match versus Stone Prince’s Men)
