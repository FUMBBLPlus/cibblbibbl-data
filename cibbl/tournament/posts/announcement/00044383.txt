Vile Cauldron



Location: Naggaroth.

Participants:
- A selection of teams approved to be competing for the honour of the Vile Cauldron, churned deeply in the dark dungeons of the witch elves, in the dark lands of Naggaroth.

Expected entrants: 12.

Tournament format: Swiss, 5 rounds.

Special rules:
- First round is seeded according to team rating.

Prestige Points are awarded as follows:
• Vile Cauldron 4th place: +2
• Vile Cauldron 3rd place: +3
• Vile Cauldron 2nd place: +5
• Vile Cauldron 1st place: +8
